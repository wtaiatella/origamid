Weight varies geographically. On average, European wolves weigh 38.5 kg; North American wolves, 36 kg; Indian and Arabian wolves, 25 kg.[7] Although rare, wolves weighing over 77 kg have been found in Alaska, Canada,[8] and the former Soviet Union.
